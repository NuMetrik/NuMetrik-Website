The Measurement Gap

Slurry environments — the core of mineral processing — have historically been a blind spot for industrial measurement. Intrusive instruments wear and fail. Nuclear gauges require radiological licensing and disposal programs. The combination of non-intrusive, real-time, and multi-parameter measurement in slurry has not been commercially available. the measurement system is the first credible solution in this space.

NuMetrik's Wedge

Three properties, simultaneously, in a single clamp-on installation:

1. Non-intrusive — clamp-on; no process contact; no shutdown to install or maintain
2. Real-time — continuous, timestamped, DCS-integrated measurement; not a lab result
3. Multi-parameter — flow, wall thickness, temperature today; density, PSD, void fraction on the validated roadmap

Section 2

Technology & Differentiation

Why Our Measurement System

The measurement physics are not new — ultrasonic flow measurement has been deployed in nuclear power, water utilities, and petrochemical applications for decades. What's new is: (1) the application to mineral processing slurry conditions, (2) the non-intrusive format at mining-relevant pipe diameters and pressures, and (3) the roadmap to multi-parameter characterization from a single sensor installation.

our technology partner has built and validated this measurement technology against nuclear-industry standards — the most demanding measurement certification environment available. That proof base is the competitive moat. No startup is replicating decade-long nuclear industry validation in a competitive timeframe.

Non-Intrusive Advantage

✓ No wear — nothing in contact with the abrasive slurry
✓ No calibration drift from process contact
✓ No shutdown required for installation, maintenance, or replacement
✓ No nuclear source — eliminates an entire regulatory and disposal liability
