Building the process intelligence platform for natural resource industries — starting with flow measurement in mineral processing.

Natural resource operations run complex processes with incomplete information. NuMetrik is building the platform that changes that: continuous, non-intrusive measurement of what is in the process, connected to where decisions are made, reconciled into a trusted operational picture.

The Problem

Process Uncertainty Has a Cost

All mineral processing plants have some instrumentation. What is harder to achieve is measurement that is continuous, accurate, and complete enough to act on with confidence. That uncertainty compounds across the circuit.

Unplanned downtime

Equipment wear and performance degradation accumulate gradually. With limited process visibility, the signal is often missed until it becomes a failure rather than a warning.

Conservative throughput margins

Operating under uncertainty means operating conservatively. Throughput is held below its potential because the data to justify a tighter target is not reliably there.

Decisions on delayed data

Manual sampling and lab turnaround place hours between a process event and a usable result. The data arrives accurate, but describing conditions that have already changed.

The root cause is consistent: the absence of reliable, real-time visibility into process conditions.

Industries

Starting in Mining. Built for Natural Resources.

NuMetrik's current commercial focus is mineral processing, the industry where we are building validated performance data and initial commercial deployments. The acoustic measurement physics apply broadly across industries where difficult process streams flow through pipes.

Current Focus

Mineral Processing & Mining

Flotation circuits, hydrocyclone feed, tailings transfer, where continuous non-intrusive flow and density measurement delivers measurable impact on recovery, throughput, and operating cost.

Oil Sands & Heavy Oil

Tailings and process streams where high-density slurry measurement is critical for mass balance, pipeline integrity, and environmental compliance.

Vision-stage. Not a current commercial focus.

Cement & Aggregate

Slurry pipelines in cement and aggregate operations (wash water circuits, limestone slurry), where intrusive instruments suffer accelerated wear.

Vision-stage. Not a current commercial focus.

Water & Wastewater

Municipal and industrial wastewater treatment involves sludge streams with density and flow measurement requirements where non-intrusive measurement reduces maintenance burden significantly.

Vision-stage. Not a current commercial focus.

Pulp & Paper

Pulp suspension density and flow measurement are instrumentation challenges with close parallels to mineral slurry measurement.

Vision-stage. Not a current commercial focus.

Dredging & Chemical Processing

High-abrasion, variable-concentration slurry pipelines (dredge operations and chemical plant transfer streams), where continuous reliable measurement is difficult with conventional instruments.

Vision-stage. Not a current commercial focus.
