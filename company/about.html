Company

Building the Process Intelligence Platform for Natural Resource Industries

NuMetrik Systems is a Canadian company on a mission to give natural resource operations the continuous, accurate process data they need to make real decisions — starting with mineral processing, where the measurement problem is most acute.

Our Mission

Natural Resource Operations Deserve Real Data.

Across mining, oil sands, cement, water treatment, and other natural resource industries, operations run complex processes with incomplete information. The variables that govern efficiency, environmental performance, and safety are either unmeasured, measured intermittently by manual sampling, or measured by instruments that wear out, require radioactive sources, or simply cannot survive the process environment.

NuMetrik exists to change that. We are building a platform — Process Intelligence, Connectivity, Certainty — that delivers continuous, accurate, trusted process data from the sensor to the decision. The physics generalise broadly. The application work is disciplined and sequential.

Our first entry point is mineral processing: grinding circuits, flotation, tailings — where slurry flows through kilometres of pipe and the measurement challenges are most acute, most consequential, and least well served by existing instrumentation. We are proving the platform here first, with the rigour required to take it further.

Where We Are

Early-Stage. Honest About It. Building in the Right Order.

NuMetrik is an early-stage company. The measurement technology at the core of our Process Intelligence solution has been validated in demanding nuclear industry environments — that validation record is the foundation we are building on, not a prototype. We are now applying it to mineral processing conditions through an active R&D program.

We are transparent about what is proven, what is in validation, and what is on the roadmap. The first commercial pilots in mineral processing are the immediate goal. Expansion to adjacent natural resource sectors follows as the application record is built.

What We Stand For

Three Operating Principles

Precision

We hold ourselves to the same standards as the technology we commercialise — independently validated, and transparent about what is proven versus what is on the roadmap. We do not claim capabilities we have not demonstrated.

Transparency

We are explicit about what the platform delivers today and what remains in active development. Pilot partners know exactly what they are getting, what the validation targets are, and why each milestone matters. No surprises.

Impact

Giving process engineers continuous, accurate data to actually control their circuits — reducing waste, improving safety, and producing a defensible record for compliance and reporting. Those are the outcomes we are optimising for.
