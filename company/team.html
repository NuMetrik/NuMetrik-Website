The Partners Who Make the Platform Possible

NuMetrik brings mining domain expertise and market access. Our partners bring proven technology. Together we're building something none of us could reach alone.

our technology partner Logo
Measurement Technology
our technology partner

Developer and manufacturer of the measurement system technology. our technology partner's technology has been validated in nuclear power generation environments — establishing a proof base that NuMetrik is extending into mineral processing. our technology partner provides hardware, calibration methodology, and nuclear-industry validation record.

Larch Logo
Connectivity Partner · Pillar II
Larch Networks

Marvell-based industrial Ethernet switches running Hardened SONiC NOS. In the NuMetrik ecosystem, Larch is the connectivity layer (Pillar II) — moving measurement data from the sensor edge through plant aggregation to the cloud historian, with native PTP time sync, PoE++ sensor power, and OT-grade cybersecurity. Marvell-certified since 2011; SONiC Foundation member since 2018.

RIF Logo
Process Reconciliation
RIF (Research in Flow)

Sensor fusion and process data reconciliation. RIF's methodology takes multiple measurement streams — from the measurement system and other instruments in the circuit — and produces a single trusted operational picture. Essential for control-grade and ESG-grade reporting where multiple sensors must be reconciled to a single value.
