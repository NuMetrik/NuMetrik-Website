The Instrumentation Case

Why the measurement system belongs in your instrumentation spec

Non-intrusive = lower installation cost

Clamp-on installation eliminates pipe modification, flanging, and process isolation from the design budget. No shutdowns required in the installation schedule. Lower CAPEX assumption for instrumentation line items vs. intrusive alternatives.

Real-time = lower lab cost assumption

Continuous measurement reduces the sampling frequency required in your OPEX model. Fewer lab samples per shift, or per week, means lower reagent, personnel, and turnaround costs in your operating cost assumptions.

Clamp-on = retrofittable

For brownfield retrofits, the measurement system installs on existing pipe without redesign. No new pipe spools, no additional space requirements, no process interruption during retrofit installation.
