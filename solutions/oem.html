Partnership Models

Two Ways to Work Together

Integration Model

Embed the measurement system as a bundled capability in your product line. Your equipment ships with the measurement system pre-mounted or pre-specified — your customer receives an instrumented system, not just a piece of equipment. NuMetrik and our technology partner provide technical integration support.

Best for: OEMs who want a differentiated, instrumented product offering. Requires engineering collaboration on mounting and data integration.

Channel Model

Recommend and co-sell the measurement system to the plants you already supply. You have the relationships — NuMetrik has the measurement technology. A channel arrangement lets you add measurement intelligence to your offering without product engineering investment.

Best for: OEMs with strong plant relationships who want to expand their solution offering. Lower commitment, faster to market.
